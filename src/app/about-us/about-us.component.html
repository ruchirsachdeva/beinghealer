Anita Sachdeva: Our Founder

With over 20 years of experience, Anita has been a guiding force in the educational sector and spiritual healing. Her journey in spirituality and leadership roles has profoundly impacted many lives. As a Reiki master and clinical hypnotherapist, Anita has helped numerous individuals achieve inner peace and overcome mental, emotional, and spiritual challenges.
