Refund Policy

Refund Policy

At Being Healer, we are committed to the satisfaction of our clients. This Refund Policy explains the terms under which you may be eligible for a refund.

Eligibility for Refund

Refunds are processed based on the following criteria:
  • If you are not satisfied with your first session, you may be eligible for a full refund.
  • Refunds for multi-session packages are available if you cancel within a specified time frame (e.g., 48 hours before the next session).
  • No refunds are available once a session has been completed.

Process for Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us within 2 hours of your session. Provide your name, session details, and the reason for the refund request.

Refund Processing

Refund requests are processed within 7 business days. Refunds are credited to the original method of payment.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund within 7 business days, first check your bank account again. Contact your credit card company, as it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. If you’ve done this and still have not received your refund, please contact us here.

Exceptions

Note that certain circumstances may be exempt from refunds. For example, discounted or promotional services may not be refundable.

Changes to Our Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. We encourage our clients to review it regularly.
